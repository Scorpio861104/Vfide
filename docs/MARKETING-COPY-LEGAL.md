## FAQs (Legal & Honest)

```markdown
## Frequently Asked Questions

### What are VFIDE tokens?
VFIDE tokens are utility tokens that provide access to protocol features including:
governance voting, zero-fee payments, network participation, and ProofScore benefits.
They are NOT investment securities or financial instruments.

### Why should I buy VFIDE?
Buy VFIDE ONLY if you want to:
- Vote on DAO governance proposals
- Use zero-fee payment network for commerce
- Operate network infrastructure for compensation
- Build ProofScore reputation for benefits

Do NOT buy VFIDE for:
- Investment or speculation
- Expected price appreciation
- Passive income
- "Getting rich quick"

### Will VFIDE price go up?
We make NO promises or predictions about future token price. Price may 
increase, decrease, or go to zero. Secondary market price depends on 
supply, demand, adoption, and speculation - which we cannot control.

Purchase only for utility use, not as investment.

### What are the risks?
Significant risks include:
- Total loss of purchase amount
- Token value may go to zero
- Smart contract bugs possible
- Protocol may fail to achieve adoption
- Regulatory landscape may change
- No guarantee of any value retention

Only purchase what you can afford to lose completely.

### Is this an investment?
NO. VFIDE tokens are utility tokens for protocol use, not investment 
securities. Do not purchase expecting profits. Purchase only if you need 
VFIDE for governance, payments, or network participation.

### What are the commitment periods?
Founding (180d), Oath (90d), Public (optional) commitments prevent 
immediate dumping and align participants with protocol success. You can 
use all utility features immediately (voting, payments, etc.), but cannot 
transfer externally until commitment ends.

This is anti-dump protection, not securities lock-up.

### Can I sell VFIDE later?
After your commitment period, you can transfer VFIDE freely including 
trading on DEX or selling peer-to-peer. However, we make NO guarantee 
regarding liquidity, price, or ability to sell.

### Who controls the protocol?
DAO (token holders) control the protocol through governance voting. No 
central team can make unilateral decisions. This is genuine decentralization, 
not marketing.

### What happens to purchase proceeds?
Presale proceeds are allocated transparently:
- **50%** - Liquidity Pool (locked on-chain, verifiable)
- **15%** - Security Audit (report published publicly)
- **15%** - Founder Reimbursement (pre-launch development costs)
- **10%** - Operations & Maintenance
- **10%** - Marketing & Growth

VFIDE was fully built and audited before the presale. The founder reimbursement 
covers work already completed, not future promises.

Founder token allocation: 50M VFIDE from DevReserveVestingVault (vested over 36 months 
with 60-day cliff).

### Is this legal?
VFIDE tokens are structured as utility tokens, not securities. We've 
consulted legal counsel and designed everything to comply with USA law. 
However, YOU must ensure compliance in YOUR jurisdiction.

This is not legal advice. Consult your own attorney.
```
