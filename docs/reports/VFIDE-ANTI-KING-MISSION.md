# VFIDE Anti-King Mission

## Core Philosophy

> **"vfide is an anti king system. we are not trying to make people rich. this is not a playground for whales we are here for the forgotten to protect not make rich. we stay fair to everyone integrity is the only power never funds"**

VFIDE exists to **protect the forgotten**, not enrich the powerful.

---

## What VFIDE Is

### Primary Mission
- **Protect small businesses** from platform abuse (chargebacks, fraud, unfair fees)
- **Level the playing field** between buyers and sellers (escrow-based fairness)
- **Community-owned governance** (handed off to the people, not controlled by founders)
- **Fair compensation for service** (not wealth accumulation)
- **Integrity-based trust** (ProofScore measures behavior, not money)

### Design Principles
1. **Zero merchant fees** - Small businesses shouldn't pay to survive
2. **Escrow protection** - Fair for both buyers and sellers
3. **ProofScore meritocracy** - Trust earned through behavior, not purchased
4. **DAO governance** - Community makes decisions, not kings
5. **Sustainable economics** - Self-funding without extraction
6. **Anti-whale** - No mechanism favors large holders over small ones

---

## What VFIDE Is NOT

### Anti-Patterns (Explicitly Rejected)
- ❌ **NOT a wealth-building scheme** - No staking APY for passive income
- ❌ **NOT a playground for whales** - No large holder advantages
- ❌ **NOT extractive governance** - DAO serves, doesn't take revenue share
- ❌ **NOT insider enrichment** - No mechanisms to make DAO members rich
- ❌ **NOT about getting rich** - Fair compensation only, not accumulation
- ❌ **NOT a "yield farm"** - No unsustainable reward emissions
- ❌ **NOT centralized** - Handed off to community, founders step back

## Economic Model (Anti-King)

### Burn Split: 25% Burn / 50% Treasury / 25% Sanctum

**OLD MODEL (Rejected):**
- 50% permanently burned
- 25% treasury
- 25% Sanctum charities
- Problem: Treasury income too low to sustain long-term

**NEW MODEL (Anti-King):**
- **25% permanently burned** (deflationary, but sustainable)
- **50% ecosystem treasury** (increased for long-term sustainability)
- **25% Sanctum charities** (humanitarian support unchanged)

### Why 50% Treasury?

The treasury (50% of all ProofScore fees) funds:
1. **Fair DAO service stipends** - 100 VFIDE/month (~$1k) for time spent
2. **Feeless merchants** (ProofScore ≥750) - Treasury pays their fees
3. **Gas subsidies** - 50-100 tx/month per merchant
4. **Operations** - Infrastructure, security, development
5. **Emergency reserves** - System stability

**What it does NOT fund:**
- ❌ Staking APY (wealth accumulation)
- ❌ Revenue share (insider enrichment)
- ❌ Large bounties (attracts wrong people)
- ❌ Whale incentives (unfair advantage)

## DAO Incentive Model (Anti-King)

### Service Deposit (10k VFIDE)
- **Purpose:** Accountability bond, not investment
- **Returned:** 100% if leave honorably
- **Forfeited:** 100% if act corruptly (zero tolerance)
- **NOT:** A staking mechanism to earn yield

### Monthly Stipend (100 VFIDE ~$1k)
- **Purpose:** Fair compensation for time spent
- **Amount:** Covers opportunity cost only (~$12k/year)
- **NOT:** Passive income or wealth-building
- **Philosophy:** Pay people fairly for service, don't make them rich

### Zero Transaction Fees (While Serving)
- **Purpose:** Practical benefit, not wealth
- **Mechanism:** `systemExempt` flag while active DAO member
- **Removed:** Immediately when leave or slashed
- **Fair:** All DAO members get same benefit (no whale advantage)

### ProofScore Boost (+5/month)
- **Purpose:** Reputation reward, not monetary
- **Effect:** Lower transaction fees, access to governance
- **Decay:** ProofScore can decrease if behavior worsens
- **NOT:** Tradeable, sellable, or extractable value

### What We Removed (And Why)

| ❌ Rejected Feature | Why It's Anti-Mission |
|---------------------|----------------------|
| **12% APY Staking** | Creates king class (passive income), unsustainable, requires separate vault allocation |
| **Revenue Share (10%)** | Enriches insiders, extractive governance, conflicts with "serve the forgotten" |
| **Large Bounties (100 VFIDE)** | Attracts wrong people (profit-seekers, not service-minded) |
| **Performance Bonuses** | Wealth accumulation disguised as merit pay |
| **$70k/year earnings** | Makes DAO members "whales", betrays forgotten people |

## Protection Mechanisms (Anti-Corruption)

### 1. Service Deposit (Accountability)
- 10k VFIDE bond required to join
- 100% forfeited if corrupt (zero tolerance)
- Cannot be withdrawn during active disputes
- Sent to treasury (funds honest member stipends)

### 2. Multi-Sig Guardian (Prevents Rogue DAO)
- 3 of 5 signatures required for proposals
- 48-hour timelock (community review)
- 20% veto power (high-trust merchants)
- Code verification (bytecode hash matching)
- Contract whitelist (only approved types)

### 3. Community Veto (20% ProofScore Weight)
- High-trust merchants can block malicious proposals
- Requires collective 20% of all ProofScore weight
- Only works if many honest merchants unite
- Prevents single whale veto abuse

### 4. Slashing (Zero Tolerance)
- 100% deposit forfeited if malicious
- Immediate removal from DAO
- systemExempt revoked (no more zero fees)
- ProofScore penalty (reputation damage)

### 5. Transparent Logging
- All DAO actions logged to ProofLedger
- Public audit trail (cannot hide corruption)
- Permanent record (immutable)
- Community can review all decisions

## Merchant Benefits (Anti-Extraction)

### Zero Fees (Core Promise)
- Merchants pay **$0** in platform fees
- ProofScore fees only apply to token transfers (NOT commerce)
- Commerce contracts marked `systemExempt` (automatic bypass)
- Only zkSync gas fees (~$0.01-0.10 per transaction)

### Escrow Protection
- 10% dispute deposit from buyers (not merchants)
- Merchant gets full payment if honest
- Deposit returned to buyer if no issues
- Bad buyers forfeit deposit (funds protection system)

### Gas Subsidies (High-Trust Merchants)
- Score ≥750: Treasury pays transaction fees
- 50-100 transactions/month covered
- Users pay nothing (truly feeless experience)
- Rewards good behavior, not wealth

### Fair Dispute Resolution
- 3-person arbitration panel (ProofScore weighted)
- Evidence-based decisions (not "he who pays more")
- Penalties scale: 1st dispute = -20, 2nd = -100, 3rd = -300 + delist
- Cannot buy your way out (integrity required)
