## 🔒 **FRAUD PREVENTION MECHANISMS**

### **1. Dispute Deposits (NEW)**
- **Buyer**: Must put 10% of order value at risk
- **Seller**: Must put 1000 VFIDE upfront at risk
- **Result**: Both parties have skin in the game, reduces frivolous actions

### **2. Escalating Penalties (NEW)**
- **First offense**: Small penalty (-5 to -20)
- **Second offense**: Medium penalty (-50 to -100)
- **Third+ offense**: Massive penalty (-200 to -300)
- **Result**: One mistake is forgiven, repeated abuse is crushed

### **3. Serial Offender Tracking (NEW)**
- **Buyer**: >5 disputes in 90 days = permanent ban
- **Seller**: 3 disputes lost = permanent delist
- **Result**: Cannot stay in system by repeatedly scamming

### **4. Withdrawal Locks (NEW)**
- **Merchant**: Cannot withdraw funds during active disputes
- **Result**: Cannot drain vault and disappear during dispute

### **5. History Tracking (NEW)**
- **All disputes tracked permanently (on-chain)**
- **Disputes won/lost tracked separately**
- **Recent disputes (90 days) tracked**
- **Result**: Cannot hide past behavior, reputation matters

### **6. Insurance Funds (NEW)**
- **Buyer Protection Fund**: Pays refunds if merchant vault empty
- **Seller Protection Fund**: Compensates for false claims
- **Result**: Both sides protected even in extreme cases
