
# THE SEER — FUNCTIONS & RESPONSIBILITIES (COPY-READY)

**The Seer** is the analytical and guidance system of VFIDE.
Its role is to examine on-chain actions, translate them into trust signals, and explain to the user exactly how their behavior affects their ProofScore and trust category.

The Seer has three core responsibilities:

---

## 1. Behavior Interpretation

The Seer studies all significant activity connected to a wallet or vault, including:

- Vault usage patterns
- Timing, size, and frequency of withdrawals
- Large-movement patterns (steady accumulation vs sudden dumping)
- GuardianLock actions
- PanicGuard triggers
- Recovery events (Chain-of-Return, Next-of-Kin)
- Staking behavior and consistency
- Governance participation
- Academy completion
- Earned badges, scrolls, and relics
- Merchant payments
- Long-term holding and stability trends

From these signals, the Seer forms a complete picture of how safe, reliable, and consistent the user behaves inside the VFIDE ecosystem.

---

## 2. ProofScore Calculation & Risk Categorization

The Seer converts behavior signals into a numeric ProofScore ranging from 0 to 1000.
This score is composed from four weighted groups:

- Stability – long-term holding, vault usage, steady patterns
- Safety – guardian setup, minimal emergency actions, responsible withdrawal habits
- Participation – staking, voting, completing Academy lessons
- Incident Impact – recovery events, repeated locks, suspicious activity patterns

After computing these parts, the Seer assigns the user to a trust category:

- Critical (0–199)
- High Risk (200–399)
- Moderate (400–599)
- Good (600–799)
- Excellent (800–1000)

This category is visualized as a trust sigil ring on the VFIDE dashboard.

---

## 3. Guidance, Warnings, and Scenario Forecasting

The Seer explains why the user received their score and how to improve it. It provides:

### A. Score Explanation
Clear reasoning behind the user’s trust status, such as:

- “Long-term staking increased your stability rating.”
- “Multiple large withdrawals in a short period lowered your safety score.”
- “Academy lessons completed boosted participation points.”

### B. Recommendations
Actionable steps to strengthen the user’s trust position:

- Add more guardians
- Reduce rapid withdrawal cycles
- Stake longer to increase stability
- Avoid unnecessary emergency actions
- Maintain steady, consistent behavior patterns

### C. Dark Trust Scenario Simulator
A safe forecasting tool that shows how hypothetical actions would affect trust:

- Dumping a large amount at once
- Performing repeated large withdrawals
- Triggering recovery events
- Increasing vault protections
- Extending staking durations

These are educational simulations only — no real transactions happen.

---

# SUMMARY

The Seer interprets on-chain behavior, generates a ProofScore using transparent rule-based logic, assigns a trust category, and explains exactly why the user received that rating. It warns against risky patterns, highlights positive behavior, and shows how future actions would raise or lower trust. The Seer exists to guide users toward safer habits, better protection, and long-term stability within the VFIDE ecosystem.
