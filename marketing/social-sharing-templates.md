## 📋 Best Practices

1. **Always include your badge number** - "#2,847" creates scarcity FOMO
2. **Show the math** - "0.8% vs 3%" is more powerful than "lower fees"
3. **Make it about the journey** - "Took 6 months" builds narrative
4. **Tag @VFIDE** - Community retweets active members
5. **Use the badge emoji** - 🏛️🏆💎🛡️⚡ help posts stand out
6. **Share progress, not just wins** - "35/50" creates anticipation
