# VFIDE Badge System V2 - Philosophy-Aligned Credentials

> "Trust badges earned through actions, not purchased with wealth"

## Overview

The badge system recognizes and rewards specific contributions, achievements, and sustained excellence in the VFIDE ecosystem. Every badge must be **earned through actions**, never purchasable with tokens.

---

## Badge Categories

### 1. 🏁 **Pioneer & Foundation** (Historical Recognition)
*Commemorative badges for early ecosystem support*

#### `PIONEER` (+30 points, Permanent)
- **Earned by**: First 10,000 users to join VFIDE
- **Requirements**: Just sign up early
- **Value**: 20 VFIDE bonus (one-time) + commemorative status
- **Philosophy**: Rewards early trust in the vision
- **Contract**: PromotionalTreasury.sol (already implemented)

#### `GENESIS_PRESALE` (+40 points, Permanent)
- **Earned by**: Participated in initial presale
- **Requirements**: Bought VFIDE during launch presale period
- **Value**: Historical supporter recognition
- **Philosophy**: Rewarding early financial commitment to project

#### `FOUNDING_MEMBER` (+50 points, Permanent)
- **Earned by**: First 1,000 users to reach 800+ ProofScore
- **Requirements**: Achieve elite status before milestone reached
- **Value**: Elite pioneer - combined early adoption + excellence
- **Philosophy**: The original high-trust community

---

### 2. 🎯 **Activity & Participation** (Engagement Recognition)
*Rewards for consistent ecosystem participation*

#### `ACTIVE_TRADER` (+20 points, Renewable: 90 days)
- **Earned by**: 50+ commerce transactions in 90 days
- **Requirements**: Minimum $50 equivalent per transaction
- **Renewal**: Automatically renews if activity continues
- **Philosophy**: Regular ecosystem usage matters

#### `GOVERNANCE_VOTER` (+25 points, Renewable: 180 days)
- **Earned by**: Voted on 10+ DAO proposals
- **Requirements**: Must have voted within last 6 months
- **Renewal**: Vote on 5 more proposals every 6 months
- **Philosophy**: Active governance participation

#### `POWER_USER` (+40 points, Renewable: 90 days)
- **Earned by**: Achieved diversification bonus (3+ activity types)
- **Requirements**: 
  - Voted in DAO
  - Made 10+ payments
  - Received 5+ payments (merchant activity)
  - Given 3+ endorsements
- **Philosophy**: True ecosystem engagement across all features

#### `DAILY_CHAMPION` (+15 points, Renewable: 30 days)
- **Earned by**: Transaction every single day for 30 consecutive days
- **Requirements**: At least 1 on-chain action daily
- **Renewal**: Maintain daily streak
- **Philosophy**: Dedication and consistency

#### `TRUSTED_ENDORSER` (+30 points, Permanent)

- **Philosophy**: Rewards excellent judgment in endorsing others

#### `COMMUNITY_BUILDER` (+35 points, Permanent)

- **Requirements**: Track referrals who achieve meaningful participation
- **Philosophy**: Growing the honest community

#### `PEACEMAKER` (+25 points, Permanent)
- **Earned by**: Resolved 3+ disputes favorably through mediation
- **Requirements**: DAO verification of dispute resolution contributions
- **Philosophy**: Conflict resolution strengthens ecosystem

#### `MENTOR` (+30 points, Renewable: 365 days)
- **Earned by**: Helped 5+ new users reach 540+ ProofScore
- **Requirements**: Mentee verification + sustained scores
- **Philosophy**: Teaching integrity and system usage

#### `INSTANT_SETTLEMENT` (+20 points, Renewable: 90 days)

- **Requirements**: Maintain high-trust status
- **Benefit**: Reduces settlement from 7 days to instant
- **Philosophy**: Elite merchants deserve immediate access to earnings

#### `ZERO_DISPUTE` (+25 points, Renewable: 180 days)

- **Requirements**: Perfect track record as merchant or buyer

- **Philosophy**: Second chances for reformed users

#### `GUARDIAN` (+40 points, Permanent)
- **Earned by**: ProofScore never dropped below 700 for 2+ years
- **Requirements**: Sustained elite status
- **Philosophy**: Unwavering trustworthiness

### 7. 🎓 **Education & Contribution** (Knowledge Sharing)
*Recognition for teaching and improving VFIDE*

- **Earned by**: Created 5+ educational content pieces (videos, guides, tutorials)
- **Requirements**: DAO approval for quality
- **Philosophy**: Growing collective knowledge

#### `CONTRIBUTOR` (+40 points, Permanent)
- **Earned by**: Made meaningful code, design, or content contribution

- **Earned by**: Reported security vulnerability
- **Requirements**: Severity-based points (Critical: 100, High: 50, Medium: 30, Low: 20)

#### `TRANSLATOR` (+25 points, Permanent)
- **Earned by**: Translated docs/UI to new language
- **Requirements**: Complete translation package

---

### 8. 🎨 **Badge Proposals** (Community-Created)
*Bottom-up credentials proposed by high-trust users*

#### `PROPOSED_*` (Variable points, Variable duration)
- **Created by**: Any user with 800+ ProofScore
- **Process**: 
  1. User proposes badge with reason
  2. 10 high-trust users support
  3. DAO final approval
- **Examples**:
  - `CHARITY_CHAMPION` - Donated to cause
  - `NFT_ARTIST` - Created VFIDE-themed art
  - `MEME_LORD` - Viral marketing content
- **Philosophy**: Community defines what matters

## Philosophy Enforcement

### ❌ Badges That Will NEVER Exist

1. **`WHALE` / `BIG_HOLDER`** - Wealth recognition violates core principle
2. **`VIP`** - Implies purchased status
3. **`PREMIUM`** - No paid tiers
4. **`STAKER_PRO`** - Capital-based advantage
5. **`EARLY_INVESTOR`** - Genesis Presale already covers this, no extra "investor" privilege

### ✅ Every Badge Must:

1. **Be earned through actions** - Not bought
2. **Encourage good behavior** - Not just participation
3. **Be achievable by anyone** - Regardless of holdings
4. **Add meaningful value** - To score or permissions
5. **Expire when appropriate** - To ensure ongoing quality

## Summary

**27 pre-defined badges** across 8 categories, all aligned with VFIDE's "integrity over wealth" philosophy.

**Key Features:**
- 14 permanent badges (achievements, historical milestones)
- 13 renewable badges (ongoing quality standards)
- Variable points (15-100) based on difficulty/impact
- Community-driven badge creation system
- Zero wealth-based badges

**Result:** A comprehensive credential system that rewards **actions, trust, and contributions** - never capital.

The badge system transforms ProofScore from a simple number into a **rich reputation profile** showing exactly what each user has accomplished in the VFIDE ecosystem. 💎
